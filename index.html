It's not just Coffee
It's Starbucks

The Coffee Belt is divided into three main growing regions: Latin America, Africa and Asia / Pacific. We responsibly source, roast and blend coffees from each of these three regions to bring you unique coffees. Just like tea or wine, coffee gets much of its flavor from where it comes from. Each of these growing regions has different growing conditions and traditions that affect the overall flavor of the coffee.
